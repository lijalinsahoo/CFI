Children’s Home

    CFI takes care of 350 plus children, including orphans, in the New Life Children’s Home on campus. If it were not for the children’s home, hundreds of India’s children would still be living on the streets with no hope of a life free of danger, starvation, begging, or even slavery. Many have no parents, others have only one parent, and several are just not able to financially provide for their children.

Public School

    Nava Jeevan Public School is affiliated with the Council for I.S.C. Examinations, New Delhi. A first class education in a Christ-centered environment is offered from kindergarten through tenth grade. Students include not only the orphans on campus, but also Hindu and Muslim children in the villages surrounding the campus. These families bring their children to CHRIST FOR INDIA to education them, even though they know the children will receive a Christian education. The Nava Jeevan Christian Junior College on campus allows students to complete their 11th and 12th grade education in a Christian environment. It provides the opportunity for faculty to bring a Christian witness and to help shape college youth.
